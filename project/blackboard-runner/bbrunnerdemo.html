Blackboard Runner Game

Blackboard Runner is a side-scrolling single player game for IPhone/ IPad. The player can control the behavior of the character through the screen of the device. The whole scene is based on a blackboard. The character was drawn on the blackboard. He/ She keeps running to avoid being erased from the blackboard. A blackboard eraser chases the character all the time. The game ends when the eraser catches the character.
